THE BRIDGEFORD SERVICES


Mphushe services will be enjoyed by all those who registered to be our clients, especially Loan-services. Non-Loan services will be offered to anyone who needs them as there will not be any financial ties to them. Loan services are for registered clients who are capable of paying back the loans. The amount of loans offered and payment period will vary from one client to another depending on their financial status.

In the same way, Mphushe! is a product that was designed to assist a client who is experiencing an unexpected financial need, and requires a financial “push start” so that they may get their personal finances back on track.

LOAN CLIENTS

Name of Process: CLIENT REGISTERATION:
Process Purpose: The purpose of client registration is to ensure that we
have a pool of clients we work with and to acquire all the
relevant information that will be required before administering
a loan to an individual.
Process Scope: This process pertains only to individuals who have a regular
income/salary and an active bank account.
Process Input: The process input for client registration is the personal
details and financial details of an individual who is interested
in our services.
Process Flow: 1.Bridgeford does an agreement with a company to offer
services to their employees.
2.Interested employees sign up for Mphushe services.
3.Registered employees’ information is stored into the
Mphushe database for future reference.
Process Output: Output for the process is a database of clients registered
with Bridgeford.

NON-LOANS CLIENTS

Unlike Loan Clients, Non-Loan clients are those that pay for the services upfront, therefore they do not need to do registration as there are no financial ties involved.

Name of Process: SERVICES DELIVERY PROCESS:
Process Purpose: The purpose of this process is to clients who are in need
of our services and are ready to pay upfront for the service,
or have the money ready to pay for the service.
Process Scope: This process applies to all clients who need our services
and can afford to pay for them without a loan.
Process Input: The process input is a client in need of services.
Process Flow: 1.Client makes a service request with a payment for the
service involved to Bridgeford.
2.Bridgeford forwards request to service providers with
payment for processing.
3. Client receives service or goods requested.
Process Output: Output for the process is a happy client who got a services
from Bridgeford.
