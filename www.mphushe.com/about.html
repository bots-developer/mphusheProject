About Bridgeford Products

Due to the client-centred business approach that is at the heart of the Bridgeford management philosophy, the company’s product portfolio exhibits the same level of dynamism as that of the needs of its clientele.

Mphushe! is our new product and the name Mphushe! was inspired by the common scenario whereby a motorist’s vehicle unexpectedly won’t start causing driver to request those around to lend a helping hand to push start the vehicle. Once the vehicle is started, the driver is able to drive to the mechanic to fix it and have it run normally.

In the same way, Mphushe! is a product that was designed to assist a client who is experiencing an unexpected financial need, and requires a financial “push start” so that they may get their personal finances back on track.

Mphushe! is a short term loan that is advanced for clients who have short term financial needs to meet essential personal or family obligations; these could be to cover funeral expenses, buy gas for the family cooker, purchase electricity, meet urgent medical bill etc. With Mphushe!, the company pays the supplier directly and recovers the advance from the client through the employer. Depending on the amount advanced and terms agreed with the client, the advance may be recoverable in 30 to 90 days.
