Welcome to

Financial Wellness and Service Advancement

Bridgeford (Pty) Ltd is a Botswana registered 100% citizen owned company that provides micro lending financial services duly licenced by the Non-Banking Financial Institutions Regulatory Authority (NBFIRA). The company aims to set itself apart from completion by continually seeking to develop unique products that relieve out clients’ short term financial challenges without setting them on a path of perpetual unproductive debt.

Our Vision

We aspire to lead the micro-lending sector in offering unique products that are aligned with the dynamic financial needs of the modern consumer.

Our Mission Statement

We provide short-term financial solutions that enhance personal debt management, relieve perpetual unproductive indebtedness and open opportunities for personal financial freedom.

Our Values

Innovation

We are committed to continually seek new ways to better meet the dynamic and diverse needs of our clientele.

Empowerment

At Bridgeford, we are a dynamic learning organisation. We have a culture of promoting and optimising opportunities for organisational continuous improvement and staff development. We extend this virtue to our clients by providing personal finance management advice.

Integrity

We shall conduct our business dealings in a transparent and fair manner, obliging to the highest professional and ethical standards.

Excellence

The Bridgeford philosophy is that ‘excellence is not a destination, but a journey’. We pursue excellence by incorporating client and stakeholder feedback in our product development and service delivery, and paying attention to detail in everything we do to deliver superior value for our clients.
